# B2B SaaS Research Project: [AI-powered SEO content production]

## Overview
This repository brings together research on the use of AI to produce SEO content in the B2B SaaS space. Instead of generic advice, it pulls insights from experienced practitioners across LinkedIn, YouTube, podcasts, and other credible sources to highlight real workflows, practical strategies, and what’s working in practice.

## Why This Topic?
I picked AI-powered SEO content production because it’s something many B2B SaaS teams are actively trying to figure out right now. There’s a ton of noise around AI and content, but not much clarity on what actually works in practice. I wanted to focus on how experienced operators are really using AI in their workflows—what they keep, what they avoid, and how they maintain quality at scale—rather than repeating generic advice

## Selected Experts & Rationale
Selected Experts & Rationale

The experts in this repository were selected based on their demonstrated experience in building, testing, and scaling SEO strategies for SaaS businesses rather than simply writing about SEO. Each expert actively shares original insights through LinkedIn, YouTube, podcasts, blogs, or industry presentations and has a proven track record in areas such as AI-assisted content creation, semantic SEO, content operations, technical SEO, or enterprise search strategy.

Selection criteria included:

Proven hands-on experience in SEO for B2B SaaS or content-led growth.
Regular publication of original research, case studies, or workflow demonstrations.
Active experimentation with AI tools such as ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or custom automation.
Strong reputation within the SEO community through agencies, SaaS companies, or independent consulting.
Content focused on practical implementation rather than theory

## Repository Structure
- `/research/sources.md`: Annotated list of all 10 experts.
- `/research/linkedin-posts/`: Scraped high-performing post data categorized by author.
- `/research/youtube-transcripts/`: API-extracted transcripts from core strategy videos.
